Moth Collection

The above two are of a hummingbird moth that wandered into our workshop to get out of the rain.

These two are of a female io moth. It was easy to figure out that it was female since the males are bright yellow.

These are pictures of an apparently shy rosy maple moth. Apparently they prefer to feed on maples, which aren't native to Florida, so I'm not entirely sure how I managed to come across it.
